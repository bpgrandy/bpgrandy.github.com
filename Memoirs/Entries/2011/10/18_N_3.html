Memoirs

 

N 3

10/18/11

 

I took a nap in the middle of the day. I couldn’t shower because I was waiting on a call. It put me in this weird limbo state of decision making. Two creatures were walking side by side, one was a dog, another was similar but they were both walking on 4 legs. They were discussing something meaningful in a high level of english when they spotted me.


[drawing on two animals with an arrow that indicates gradient spotlight]


    We made eye contact  and they stopped talking and snarled, as if in a reactionary manner. The snarl startled me and I found myself fully awake on my couch. I haven’t fully dismissed the idea of an alternate reality that is the dream world. These two creatures/ souls chose to embody an animal form for whatever reason. It was clear to them that I didn’t belong and they exited me from their world with a quick snarl.         .            .        almost habitual.
