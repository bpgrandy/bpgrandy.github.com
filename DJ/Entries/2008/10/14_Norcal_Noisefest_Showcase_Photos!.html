Norcal Noisefest Showcase
The Norcal Experimental Music Festival was started in 1995. The first
event was held at the Guild Theatre in Sacramento, on November 3rd,
& 4th. Performers came from all over the western US & Canada.
2008 marks the 13th anniversary of NOISEFEST happening in the
Sacramento area.
