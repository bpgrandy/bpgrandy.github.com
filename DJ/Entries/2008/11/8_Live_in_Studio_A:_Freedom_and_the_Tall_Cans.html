Live in Studio A: Freedom and the Tall Cans

Saturday, November 8, 2008

 

Freedom and the Tall Cans - are David Wilcox, Tim Mercer, Jarred Wright, and Freedom Glory: "local mountains kids that got bored". 

Show Direct Link


Bluegrass that they say draws a large audience from kids and old people, despite the fact that they in no way intended that to be their demographic.  The music kind of speaks for itself, and I can see how little kids would enjoy it.  But these guys were super chill, I'm pretty sure a couple of them were high and apparently they asked if they could drink in the studio at some point, but overall I'd say it helped their music.
